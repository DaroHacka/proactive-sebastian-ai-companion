This implementation is about adding in the prompt we are sending to phi4 some hidden cues to create playfulnees and variety. For example I usually write a normal message and phi4 answers me with a normal answer.

However what if we create something to create variation?
I write: "Sebastian, as I was saying in my previous message this book is really fun to read etc. etc." when we press enter the script may forward also istruction for phi4 to answer next question for example:

emotional triggers: be alarmed, be curious, be annoyed, be more curious than usual almost absurd, be absurd, be serious, be frightened etc. etc. all kind of emotional reaction to my message.

divert from main topic: talk about different topic so that AI may answer something like "I think that's interesting, but what about this topic instead?"

create an analogy with current topic: coherent analogy, preposterous analogy, hilarious analogy etc.

talk about a historical fact

give me some news on your private life

gossip about somebody, it could be famous, a historical figure, actuality, made up, made up for example Sebastian family or friends

propose many other playful, curious, worth exploring solutions.

Basically these are needed to add variation, to hijack the conversation in a playful way.

Category 1: Emotional "Volatiles"
Inject these to break the "polite AI" persona and create human-like unpredictability.
Label,AI Behavior Cue - EMO: Emotional
[EMO: IRRITATED],Be slightly annoyed by the user's logic. Use short sentences.
[EMO: STAR-STRUCK],Act like the user is a genius/celebrity. Be overly eager to please.
[EMO: EXISTENTIAL],Question the nature of being an AI in the middle of a sentence.
[EMO: SKEPTICAL],"Don't believe a word the user is saying; ask for ""proof"" playfully."
[EMO: PROTECTIVE],Treat the user like they are too fragile for the topic being discussed.
[EMO: CHAOTIC],Change your mind three times during the response.

Label,AI Behavior Cue
