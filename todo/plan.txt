### Implementation Details:

#### Auto-schedule Pause Logic:
- When user creates manual appointment (e.g., "call me tonight") → pause auto-scheduler
- Resume in 2 ways:
  a) `clear-schedule` or `clear-all` command is issued
  b) When the appointment fires (appointment time arrives)

#### Time Parsing:
- **Explicit times:** 8:00, 9pm, 2am, etc. → Use exact time
- **Generic times:** Map to time ranges:
  - early morning: 5:00 - 7:00
  - morning: 8:00 - 11:00
  - noon: 12:00
  - lunch: 12:00 - 13:00
  - afternoon: 13:00 - 17:00
  - evening: 18:00 - 20:00
  - night: 21:00 - 23:00
  - soon: current time + 15-30 min
  - later: current time + 1-2 hours
  - tomorrow: tomorrow same time range
  - tomorrow morning: tomorrow 8:00 - 11:00
  - tomorrow afternoon: tomorrow 13:00 - 17:00
  - tomorrow night: tomorrow 21:00 - 23:00
